OMPIXS - VIDEOGRAPHY AND CAMERA RENTALS

Optical Motion Pictures Limited has been in the Television and Film industry in Nigeria, West Africa for over a decade and currently residing in BC Canada. Founded by Adeyemi Kolade. We are a highly skilled and creative professional who produces high-quality videos for clients on a project-by-project basis. With extensive experience in video production, we offer a wide range of services including pre-production planning, scripting, filming, editing, sound design, and color grading. We also deal in renting of Cameras for all forms of events.

Our mission is to create visually stunning videos that meet our clients' needs and exceed their expectations. We take pride in our ability to work collaboratively with clients and other professionals in the industry to bring their vision to life. With a strong work ethic and dedication to quality, we are committed to building a reputation for exceptional service and delivering outstanding results.
