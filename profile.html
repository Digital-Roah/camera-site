About Adeyemi Kolade

Adeyemi Kolade is one of Nigerian fast rising and Africans TV Technical director & director of photography, with more than ten years of experience in capturing and producing exceptional video content for TV, films, advertising, and online audio-visual platforms. He is the founder of Optical Motions Pictures (Ompixs).

Adeyemi's journey began in 2008 when he started as a TV engineering student at NTA Channel 10 Lagos, after which he moved to Lagos Network Centre and began working as a camera assistant. He quickly progressed through various roles in different production units, including sound, editing, vision mixing, associate producing, directing, and producing live entertainment shows and segments for shows like Africa Magic (AM) Express, Morning Ride, Newsline, Tales by Moonlight, and The Entertainers.

Having gained extensive experience, Adeyemi embarked on freelance work, collaborating with independent content producers and TV shows across Lagos. In 2014, he joined Blink Africa as a full-time contract staff member, working as the Director of Photography for major TV content shows on Africa Magic, such as Hustle, Truth, Unmarried, Shoot Your Shot, Date My Family Nigeria, and Dilemma.

Throughout his career, Adeyemi has worked with renowned production companies like Daydreams Productions, Lucid Production, Urban Vision, Dragon Africa, Storm360 Production, Delmedia, Red-media, The Lovelynclair TV Show, and on shows like The Voice Nigeria, Ignite Africa, The Terakulture theatre shows, Discovery +234, Throwback on Mnet, Taruwa, and Guiness Worldcup Jessey in 2010.

In 2019, Adeyemi became one of the twelve Nigerian Technical Directors on the popular reality TV show Big Brother Nigeria (BBN), directing for three consecutive seasons. He also served as the Reunion Studio Set-Up Manager and Technical Director for seasons 4-7, and worked as the Director of Photography for the AMVCA announcement and various corporate branded events and conference productions.

Adeyemi's services include video coverage for weddings, conferences, and corporate events. He specializes in documentary production, corporate profiles, TV series, reality show production, commercials, online content, live events, and multimedia. His expertise lies in bringing clients' visions to life through creative and engaging storytelling.

Recognizing the significance of social media, Adeyemi also offers social media content creation services, ensuring impactful videos that stand out on platforms like Instagram, Facebook, TikTok, and YouTube.
